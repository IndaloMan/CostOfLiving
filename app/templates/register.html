Cost of Living Tracker

What is the Cost of Living Tracker?

This is a community tool that helps Mojacar residents understand how much everyday shopping really costs. By photographing your supermarket receipts, you contribute to a shared picture of local prices — and in return you get your own personal spending dashboard.

No personal information is required. You can participate completely anonymously.
