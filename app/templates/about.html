About

Cost of Living Tracker

A private household expense tracker designed to make capturing and reviewing receipts quick and easy — especially on mobile.

How It Works

Photograph or upload a receipt and the app uses AI to extract the line items, date, and total automatically. You review, correct if needed, and confirm.

Privacy

All data is stored privately and is only accessible to members of your household. No data is shared with third parties.
