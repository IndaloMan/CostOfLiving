About

Cost of Living Tracker

A private household expense tracker designed to make capturing and reviewing receipts quick and easy — especially on mobile.

How It Works

Photograph or upload a receipt and the app uses AI to extract the line items, date, and total automatically. You review, correct if needed, and confirm.

Privacy

All data is stored privately and is only accessible to members of your household. No data is shared with third parties.

Cookies

This app uses two cookies — small files stored in your browser — both are essential for it to work:

  • Session cookie — keeps you logged in while your browser is open. It is deleted automatically when you close the browser.
  • Remember me cookie — keeps you logged in when you return on another day, so you do not have to enter your password every time. It expires after one year.

No tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies are used. The Google Translate button (in the navigation bar) may store your language preference in your browser, but this is handled entirely by Google's widget and no data is sent to this app.
