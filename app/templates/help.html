Help

Getting Started

Use the Scan menu item to photograph or upload a receipt. The app will extract the items automatically.

After reviewing the extracted data, tap Confirm to save the receipt to your account.

Viewing Your Spending

The Expenditure page shows all your confirmed receipts. Use the date filters to narrow the view.

The Reports page gives you charts and summaries of your spending over time.

Managing Your Account

Visit Account to update your details or change your password.

Need More Help?

Contact your administrator if you have questions or encounter any problems.
